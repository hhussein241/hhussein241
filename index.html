General Information

The purpose of this website is to store various bits and pieces of my life in one convenient receptacle.


My name is Hamza Hussein. I'm a 19-year-old university student in North America. My hobbies include running, boating, and sketching (of which you can view the product of in the Art section). I am studying computer science, and dabble in video games on the side. Although I intend to become a programmer, my dream job is to be part of a submarine crew. You can view the latest thing I'm fiddling with on Misc., view any of my sketches by clicking on Art, and send me feedback on how this website can be improved through Feedback.


Why Make This?

The 'Blog & Emporium' exists for a number of reasons. I took a course in basic HTML and particularly enjoyed making websites from scratch. From there I fantasized about generating cash on the side writing things like this. One of the first things you should accomplish before offering such a service is to have your own website; perhaps dedicated to a hobby and/or showcasing other websites you've made. This website is it.
